About

The path shaped
the person.

In 2020, I graduated high school into a pandemic and thru-hiked the Appalachian Trail instead of going to college. 2,193 miles, Georgia to Maine, alone. That decision became a career.

Since then: the Pacific Crest Trail, the Colorado Trail, Everest Base Camp, the Tour du Mont Blanc. 6,500+ miles across three continents and five countries. I'm based in Park City, Utah, building Expedition Excellence to bring serious wilderness experiences to the people who are ready for the real thing.

  • BBA Entrepreneurship, University of Mississippi, 3.8 GPA
  • Eagle Scout, Boy Scouts of America
  • FAA Part 107 Certified Drone Pilot
