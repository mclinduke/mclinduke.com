Ch. I
The Subject

In 2020 I graduated high school into a pandemic and thru-hiked the Appalachian Trail instead of going to college. 2,193 miles, Georgia to Maine, alone. That decision became a career.

Since then: the Pacific Crest Trail, the Colorado Trail, Everest Base Camp, the Tour du Mont Blanc. 6,500+ miles across three continents and nine countries. I'm based in Park City, Utah, building Expedition Excellence to bring serious wilderness experiences to people who are ready for the real thing.

Available for aerial cinematography — commercial, event, real estate. FAA Part 107. References on request.
