- H2: `clamp(2rem, 4vw, 3.5rem)` — Cormorant, weight 400
- H3: `clamp(1.4rem, 2.5vw, 2rem)` — Cormorant, weight 400
- Body: `1.05rem` / line-height 1.7 — Inter, weight 400
- Labels: `0.75rem` / letter-spacing 0.2em / uppercase — JetBrains Mono

## Brand Tone: Quiet Confidence

### Voice Principles
1. **Let the work speak.** Don't hype — state facts. 6,500 miles is impressive on its own.
2. **Short sentences.** No filler, no fluff, no exclamation marks.
3. **Understated luxury.** The restraint *is* the signal. If you have to explain why it's impressive, the copy failed.
4. **First person, sparingly.** Use "I" when it matters, but let the numbers and credentials carry weight.
5. **No jargon, no cringe.** Never say "epic", "awesome", "crushing it", "living my best life."

### Copy Examples

| Element     | Example                                        |
|------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Headline   | "The path shaped the person."                   |
| Tagline    | "6,500 miles. No shortcuts."                    |
| CTA        | "Begin the conversation."                       |
| Bio opener | "I've walked every mile."                       |
| Trail desc | "2,193 miles. Georgia to Maine. Five months."   |

### Words to Use
Earned, curated, deliberate, tested, proven, remote, rugged, considered, bespoke, personal

### Words to Avoid
Epic, awesome, amazing, insane, crushing it, adventure of a lifetime, bucket list, BOOK NOW

## Application Notes

- **Personal site (mclinduke.com):** Full brand guide applies directly.
- **Expedition Excellence:** Same palette and typography. Tone shifts slightly more toward exclusivity — even fewer words, more white space, "inquire" instead of "book."
- **Photography:** Let images breathe. Full-bleed where possible. No filters — the Earth & Sand palette provides enough warmth.
- **Buttons:** Sage green fill with espresso text for primary. Transparent with sage border for secondary.
