Pinhoti Trail

335 Miles
19 Days
AL → GA Route

The Pinhoti Trail is a long-distance trail in the southeastern United States, stretching 335 miles through Alabama and Georgia. I thru-hiked this trail in 19 days at the beginning of 2023 during my Christmas break at Ole Miss.

I hiked with a buddy from Birmingham whom I met in a hostel the previous summer on the Colorado Trail. He had plenty of friends in the area who brought us Chick-fil-A on trail...which is huge during a thru-hike.
