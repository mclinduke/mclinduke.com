Colorado Trail

485 Miles
~30 Days
Durango → Denver Route

During the summer of 2022, I needed another adventure. The 485-mile-long Colorado Trail felt like the perfect move after the AT. Higher elevation, more miles above the treeline, and a more remote trail were what I decided on.

This trail goes from Durango to Denver and passes near a bunch of cozy mountain towns and ski resort areas. I started on June 29 and finished on July 28. Met some incredible people & stayed in some awesome hostels along the way.
