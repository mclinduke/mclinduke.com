The path shaped
the person.

In 2020, I graduated high school into a pandemic and thru-hiked the Appalachian Trail instead of going to college. 2,193 miles, Georgia to Maine, alone. That decision became a career.

What started as a single thru-hike turned into a calling. The PCT, the Colorado Trail, Everest Base Camp, the Tour du Mont Blanc. Since then I've logged 6,500+ miles across three continents, five countries, and ten major expeditions.

Along the way I earned a BBA in Entrepreneurship from the University of Mississippi while finishing multiple trails, became FAA Part 107 certified as a commercial drone pilot, and started building a media and expedition business out of Park City, Utah.

The drone work grew out of the filmmaking I was already doing on trail. The consulting grew out of being the person people kept asking for advice. Expedition Excellence grew out of wanting to offer serious wilderness experiences to the people who are ready for the real thing.

I'm Eagle Scout, I've spent serious time in the backcountry, and I've done most of it alone. That background is what I bring to every project, every flight, and every client conversation.
