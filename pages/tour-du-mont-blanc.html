Tour du Mont Blanc

100 Miles
7 Days
3 Countries

In the summer of 2024, I hiked the Tour Du Mont Blanc trail through Switzerland, Italy, & France. 100 miles in 7 days. It was the most snow I had ever seen in my entire life. The TMB is now my new favorite trail.

I met people from all over the world hiking out there & a new friend Kat who I finished the trail with. The temperature swings were crazy. We'd wake up freezing then be burning up & sweating just 30 minutes later, then at the top of the climb, we would be shivering again.

Tons of core memories were made on this trail. Hiking into all of the little European alpine villages was always a welcomed treat and a stark contrast to the beautiful trail that seems so remote when you are hiking in it.
