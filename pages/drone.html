Common Questions

Before you book.

Do you handle the FAA paperwork?

Yes. All airspace authorizations, LAANC approvals, and waivers are handled on my end. You don't need to know the regulations. That's what the Part 107 certification is for.

What if the weather doesn't cooperate?

I monitor conditions closely and will reschedule at no charge if wind, precipitation, or visibility make the flight unsafe or the footage substandard. Utah weather moves fast. I know how to read it.

Can I get the raw footage?

Yes. Raw footage packages are available as an add-on for any project. Just let me know upfront and I'll include it in the quote.

Do you travel outside Park City?

Standard service radius is 35 miles, covering Park City, Salt Lake City, Provo, and most of the Wasatch Front. Beyond that, travel costs are quoted separately. I'm available anywhere with enough lead time.

How fast is delivery?

Standard turnaround is 48 hours for real estate and most commercial work. Weddings and cinematic projects are typically 5 business days. Same-day delivery is available for an additional fee.

What makes you different from other drone operators?

6,500+ miles on foot across Utah's backcountry means I understand terrain, light, and how the land reads from the air in a way most pilots don't. I'm a filmmaker who knows this landscape.
